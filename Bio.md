## 🏆 Featured Project

### [PlantVillage Disease Classifier](https://github.com/imadsaeed/PlantVillage-Project)

**99.77% Accuracy** | Ensemble of Tiny NN + LightGBM | 38 Plant Disease Classes

- Feature caching strategy → **15× faster training** (4 hours → 15 minutes)
- CLAHE + LAB space preprocessing for lighting normalization
- Weighted soft voting ensemble (75% Tiny NN + 25% LightGBM)
- Handles grayscale, RGBA, and multiple image formats

**Key Lesson:** Abandoned failing fine-tuning (14% accuracy), pivoted to feature caching → achieved 99.77%
